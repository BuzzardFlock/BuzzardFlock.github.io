Methods and Simulations for the Dark Energy Survey

Simulations

  • 3 10,313 sq. degree galaxy catalogs complete to r~26.5 and z=2.35
  • Each simulation built on N-body lightcones constructed on the fly
  • Galaxies are populated using ADDGALS, and include positions, velocities, observed magnitudes
  • Weak lensing statistics for all galaxies via high resolution ray-tracing using CALCLENS
  • 18 DES Y1 footprints with realistic photometric errors and photo-zs
  • Each footprint comes with simulated Gold, redMaGiC, redMaPPer, and metacalibration samples
